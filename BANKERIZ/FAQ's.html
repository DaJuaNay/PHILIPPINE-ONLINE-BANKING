Frequently Asked Questions

To create a new account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions. You will need to provide personal information and verify your identity.
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be asked to enter your email address, and we will send you instructions to reset your password.
To transfer funds, log in to your account and navigate to the "Transfer Funds" section. Follow the prompts to enter the recipient's account details and the amount you wish to transfer.
If you suspect fraudulent activity, contact our customer service immediately at 1-800-123-4567. Additionally, you can lock your account through the mobile app or online banking portal to prevent further unauthorized transactions.
